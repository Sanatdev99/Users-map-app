## Choose the Right Transport Type

### Open Transport
The most economical option. Your vehicle is transported on an open carrier trailer.

**Best for:**
- Standard sedans and SUVs
- Budget-conscious customers
- Non-luxury vehicles

**Pros:**
- Most affordable option
- Widely available
- Faster pickup times

**Cons:**
- Exposed to weather
- Visible to other drivers

### Enclosed Transport
Provides maximum protection with a fully enclosed trailer.

**Best for:**
- Luxury and exotic cars
- Classic and vintage vehicles
- High-value automobiles

**Pros:**
- Complete weather protection
- Enhanced security
- Safer for valuable vehicles

**Cons:**
- More expensive
- Limited availability

## How to Prepare Your Car for Shipping

### 1. Clean Your Vehicle
Wash your car inside and out to document its condition.

### 2. Remove Personal Items
Carriers are not liable for personal belongings left in the vehicle.

### 3. Check for Leaks
Ensure there are no fluid leaks that could cause issues during transport.

### 4. Document Condition
Take photos of your vehicle from all angles before shipping.

### 5. Disable Alarms
Turn off your car alarm to avoid issues during transport.
